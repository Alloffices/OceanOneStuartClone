Virtual Office

A Virtual Office offers you a professional business presence with all the amenities of a large corporate office. Ocean One Stuart's business identity plan provides you with total office support even if you work from your home.

A Virtual Office is ideal for entrepreneurs who are starting a new venture or sales representatives and consultants who spend only a limited time in the office.

Range of Benefits for Virtual Office Plans

  • A prestigious business address for use on your letterhead, cards and literature.
  • A friendly receptionist and professional staff to represent you.
  • A personalized telephone answering service and message center.
  • Complete mail handling and forwarding.
  • Access to a fully furnished executive office (when available), and conference room.
